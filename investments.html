Common Questions

Minimum investments vary by project type. Land banking typically starts at $25,000, while development projects may require $50,000 or more. Contact us to discuss options that fit your budget.

Timeline varies by investment type. Income properties generate monthly cash flow immediately. Land banking typically sees appreciation within 2-5 years. Development projects usually return capital within 12-24 months upon completion and sale.

We focus on high-growth markets in the Southeast United States, including Florida, Georgia, Tennessee, and the Carolinas. These regions offer strong population growth, business-friendly environments, and excellent appreciation potential.

All investors receive access to our secure investor portal with real-time updates on project progress, financial reports, and distributions. We also provide quarterly performance reports and host annual investor meetings.
